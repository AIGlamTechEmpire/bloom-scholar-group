Who is this actually for?

Rising sophomores, juniors, and seniors who are smart and capable but need more than just "study harder" advice. If your student is going through the motions without a real plan, or they're stressed about college without knowing why — this is for them.

What is Scholar Self-Mapping?

It's our core framework. Think of it like this: we excavate who your student really is (personality), match that to careers and majors that make sense, build a strategic college plan with scholarship opportunities baked in, and top it off with SAT prep that's actually tactical. Every piece builds on the one before it. Nothing is random.

How is this different from regular SAT tutoring?

Most tutoring centers just throw practice tests at your kid and hope for the best. We teach strategy — actual tactical approaches to Reading and Writing that change how they attack the test. Plus, the SAT work is connected to the bigger picture of who they are and where they're headed, so they stay motivated instead of burned out.

What's the deal with the personality profiles?

We're not talking about a 5-minute online quiz. Students go through guided assessments that reveal how they learn, what lights them up, what drains them, and where their natural strengths actually are. Then we use that data to drive everything else — career direction, college fit, even how they study for the SAT.

My kid already knows what they want to do. Do they still need this?

Honestly? Yes. Students who already have a direction get even more out of this because we validate that choice with real data, sharpen their SAT scores, and build a strategic application plan around it. And if they're wrong about the direction? Better to figure that out now than sophomore year of college.

What does my student actually walk away with?

Their full personality profile and debrief, a career pathway map, a curated college list with scholarship and summer program recommendations, an application timeline, essay topic ideas, a personalized SAT study plan, and a framework they can keep using. This isn't a one-and-done workshop — it's a toolkit for the whole journey.
